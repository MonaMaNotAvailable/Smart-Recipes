Okonomiyaki Recipe

Description: Okonomiyaki is a Japanese savory pancake dish consisting of wheat flour batter and other ingredients cooked on a teppan.

Ingredients:

  • Cabbage
  • Meat
  • Seafood
  • Okonomiyaki sauce
  • Japanese mayonnaise
  • Aonori(dried green laver or seaweed)
  • Katsuobushi(bonito flakes)
  • Gari(pickled ginger)

Cooking Steps:

  1. Mix
  2. Fry
  3. Serve
